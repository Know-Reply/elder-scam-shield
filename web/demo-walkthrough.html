Information Provenance

Who Knew What First?

The scammer doesn't know your grandson's name. But if you say it first, they'll use it like they always knew. Elder Shield tracks who revealed every fact — and whether the sender's identity is built from information the elder gave away.

Beyond Stranger Scams

Trusted Doesn't Mean Safe

The same behavioral signals — isolation, financial control, emotional manipulation — appear in elder abuse by trusted contacts. A caregiver who gradually takes over finances triggers the same velocity scoring as a stranger who builds trust over 7 days.

Referenced: Japan's Elder Abuse Prevention Act (高齢者虐待防止法, 2006) — Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare
